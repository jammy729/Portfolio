I welcome you to the site of James Yoo. I am currently attending Simon Fraser University studying Interactive Systems at faculty of School of Interactive Arts and Technologies (SIAT).

I am a front-end developer with UX and UI focused. I love to express my visual through creative coding. The combination of <programming> x design is what I am passionate about

I describe myself as a minimalist designer. Unusual, but I have developed passion and liking for minimalistic from playing competitive golf. Objective of golf is to have the least amount of strokes per round and I have always been a big fan of minimalistic approach to my swing rather than complicated mechanism. From playing golf, I have developed liking for minimalistic and that carried over to my passion for minimalistic design and life
